RelayX Product Information

Features:
- AI-powered voice calling with natural conversations
- Automatic call transcription and analysis
- Sentiment detection and outcome tracking
- Call recordings with playback
- Knowledge base for accurate responses
- Custom AI agents with different personalities
- Real-time conversation monitoring

- Pro Plan: $299/month - Unlimited calls with priority support
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing - Dedicated account manager and custom integrations

Technical Details:

- Twilio integration for reliable calling
